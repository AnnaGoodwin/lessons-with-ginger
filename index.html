Bio

Ginger Wade Holds a Bachelor Degree in Music Education from California State University Fullerton. She has taught private lessons for 10 years and has taught choir and general music both in California and Arizona for six years. She teaches privately in her home studio before school hours and also teaches lessons at Desert Ridge Music Academy in the afternoons. Ginger has a passion for education and loves adapting her teaching to various learning styles. She is an active member of the Arizona Music Educator's Association and the American Choral Director's Association.
